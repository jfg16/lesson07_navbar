About the Author

Jonathan Garcia is an Information, Communication, and Technology student who aspires to be either a web application designer, non-profit tech support, or lawyer with a concentration in multimedia. This site was created to show the benefits and downsides to social media when it comes to social movements. I did this because I am an ICT major which studies social media and how to manage it but on top of that, my dream is to work in a non-profit and this world coincides with the social activism world.
